"What does implementation actually look like?"
Phased deployment, not a big bang. Standard mid-market deployment runs 12–16 weeks in four phases: data audit and migration scoping, core CRM configuration, Agentforce agent activation, and team enablement. Each phase has defined exit criteria. You do not go live until both sides sign off on readiness.
"How does consumption-based pricing work?"

Migration is real work. We do not pretend otherwise. Salesforce Data Cloud migration tooling handles most structured data transfer — but data cleanup is yours to do. We provide a data audit in Week 1, a migration plan by Week 3, and dedicated migration support through go-live.

Frequently Asked Questions

Pricing questions, answered directly.

How does Agentforce consumption-based pricing work? ›
Agentforce is priced per conversation or action taken by an AI agent — not per seat. You pay for what your agents actually do. We offer monthly cost-cap configurations that set a hard ceiling per workspace, so you're never exposed to open-ended consumption. Before you sign, we model projected consumption based on your org size, use case mix, and historical volume so you have a reliable cost estimate — not a range.
What's included in the free trial? ›
The 30-day free trial includes full access to Starter Suite — CRM for sales, service, and marketing with basic Agentforce capabilities. No credit card required. At the end of 30 days, you choose a plan or your data is retained for 60 days while you decide. There is no automatic charge.
What does migration cost? ›
Migration is real work, and we don't pretend otherwise. Salesforce Data Cloud migration tooling handles most structured data transfer from common CRMs at no additional cost. Data cleanup is your responsibility — no vendor can do that for you. For Enterprise deployments, we include a Week 1 data audit and a Week 3 migration plan as part of the scoped deployment. Professional services fees vary by complexity and are quoted separately.
Is there a switching cost from our current CRM? ›
Yes, and we'd rather tell you upfront than have you find out mid-implementation. Migration requires time from your RevOps or IT team — typically 6–8 weeks of parallel operation before full cutover. We provide structured migration support, a data audit, and phased deployment to minimize disruption. The ROI model we build with you includes migration cost as a line item, not as a footnote.
