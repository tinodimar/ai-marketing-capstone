Common concerns, addressed directly

What mid-market buyers ask before they shortlist.

These are the three objections that come up in 80% of first conversations. We'd rather address them here than wait for your RFP.

"What does implementation actually look like?"
Phased deployment, not a big bang. Standard mid-market deployment runs 12–16 weeks in four phases: data audit and migration scoping, core CRM configuration, Agentforce agent activation, and team enablement. Each phase has defined exit criteria before the next begins.
"How does consumption-based pricing work at our scale?"
Predictable cost, not open-ended consumption. We offer cost-cap configurations that set a monthly ceiling per workspace. Your RevOps team will see actual usage dashboards, and we model projected consumption based on your org size before you commit.
"What happens to our existing data?"
Migration is real work. We do not pretend otherwise. Salesforce Data Cloud migration tooling handles the majority of structured data transfer. We provide a detailed data audit in Week 1, a migration plan by Week 3, and dedicated support through go-live.
